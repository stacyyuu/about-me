Hello! My name is Stacy.

I am an aspiring Software Developer who has just begun my journey at Code Fellows. I am so excited to learn what it takes to become a successful developer.

My experience started in the beginning of 2022, which consisted of self-studying. After a couple of weeks trying to learn by myself, I realized I would do a lot better with guidance. My goal is to become a competent developer and hopefully land a job at a company that I love!
